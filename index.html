Academic Courses

Number games

Introducing pre-math concepts to children and helping them with the basics of numeracy is a skill that needs to be taught innovatively so that our children have a strong foundation. The program "Number without Tears" will make this learning easier and more fun for your child.

Phonics sessions

Introducing reading courses to children and helping them with the basics of reading skills that need to be taught innovatively so that our children have a strong foundation. The program "Fun way to learn reading two or three letters word" will make this learning easier and fun for your child.

Mental math program

These classes will help your kid who is struggling in the mental calculation of addition to two or three-digit numbers with carryover. Mental math can also help kids understand math concepts better. Using and practicing mental math regularly helps kids improve their number sense

Language course
